What Is IRR?

The total return from the property on an annual basis considering the property is sold after 7 years of the initial investment - this captures the total income to you from both rentals and appreciation of the property.

What Is Yield?

The rental income generated from the property as a %age of its total purchase price inclusive of Interior Costs, Registration and Stamp Duties after deducting all Operating costs, Commissions and Home Owner Association Fees.
